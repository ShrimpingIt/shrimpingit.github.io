LED Clock Addon Kit

This is a bagged kit of the project-specific components to build a @ShrimpingIt LED Clock following our step by step build instructions.

Project Bundle

To make the project, you need the following @ShrimpingIt kits (or equivalent parts you have sourced yourself)...

  • This LED Clock Addon kit
  • An Alarm Clock Addon kit
  • A Shrimp Parts kit
  • Two 400-point breadboards
  • A USB UART kit

At the time of writing prepared bundles for the LED Clock project have been priced at £13.80 according to the standard pricing scheme for our bagging service.
