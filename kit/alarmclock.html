Alarm Clock Addon Kit

We sell pre-bagged components to build a @ShrimpingIt Alarm Clock, to accompany our step by step build instructions.

Project Bundle

To make the project, you need the following @ShrimpingIt kits (or equivalent parts you have sourced yourself)...

  • A 400point Breadboard (to build the circuit on)
  • A Shrimp Parts kit (microcontroller and supporting parts)
  • A UART kit (for uploading programmed behaviours to your Shrimp)
  • This Alarm Clock Addon kit

At the time of writing prepared bundles for the Alarm Clock project have been priced at £10.30 according to the standard pricing scheme for our bagging service.
